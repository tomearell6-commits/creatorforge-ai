# CreatorForge — Tutorial Video Script (end-to-end)

A complete, record-ready script for the tutorial library. Each "lesson" = one
short video (60–120s). Format per lesson: **[ON SCREEN]** = what to show
(screen recording of the real app), **[SAY]** = presenter/voiceover narration.
Record the screen actions live for trust; a presenter or avatar can narrate.

> Tone: friendly, confident, concise. Always show the real UI. Never promise a
> platform action unless it actually completes on screen.

---

## Lesson 1 — Getting Started (overview) · Category: Getting Started
**[ON SCREEN]** Landing page → click "Start Creating" → signup → dashboard.
**[SAY]** "Welcome to CreatorForge. In the next few minutes I'll show you how to
go from a single idea to a finished, published video — and how credits work so
there are no surprises. Sign up with email or Google, and you land on your
dashboard. At the top you'll see your Credit Wallet — that's what powers every
AI action. Let's create something."

## Lesson 2 — Your first AI video · Category: Create
**[ON SCREEN]** Dashboard → Create Content → AI Video Studio → pick "AI Shorts" →
enter a prompt → generate script → scenes → voiceover → images → Render Queue.
**[SAY]** "Open Create Content and choose AI Video Studio. Pick a format like AI
Shorts, describe your idea, and CreatorForge writes the script, breaks it into
scenes, adds a voiceover, and generates images automatically. When you're happy,
send it to the Render Queue to produce a finished MP4. Each step shows its credit
cost up front."

## Lesson 3 — Generate an SEO blog post & publish to WordPress · Category: SEO
**[ON SCREEN]** AI SEO Studio → enter topic → Generate → edit draft → connect a
WordPress site → Publish.
**[SAY]** "In the AI SEO Studio, type a topic and hit Generate. You get a full,
optimized article — title, meta, headings, FAQ. Connect your WordPress site once
with an Application Password, then publish or schedule straight from here."

## Lesson 4 — Run an SEO Audit · Category: SEO
**[ON SCREEN]** SEO Audit → paste URL → pick Quick Audit → Start → scores +
issues → Generate Fix Plan.
**[SAY]** "Want to know how your site is doing? Open SEO Audit, paste your URL,
and run a Quick Audit. You get scores across technical, content, performance and
indexing, a prioritized issue list, and a one-click fix plan you can act on."

## Lesson 5 — Connect your accounts · Category: Publishing
**[ON SCREEN]** Social Accounts → connect YouTube/TikTok/Instagram; WordPress
Sites → add a site.
**[SAY]** "Before publishing, connect your destinations under Social Accounts and
WordPress Sites. CreatorForge only ever posts to accounts you've connected and
authorized."

## Lesson 6 — Credits & crypto top-ups · Category: Billing
**[ON SCREEN]** Credit Wallet → breakdown (monthly/bonus/purchased) → pick a
package → choose a coin → checkout panel.
**[SAY]** "Every action costs credits, and your wallet shows exactly what you have
— monthly, bonus, and purchased. Need more? Top up instantly with crypto. Purchased
credits never expire, and you always see the cost before you generate."

## Lesson 7 — Set up Autopilot · Category: Automation
**[ON SCREEN]** Autopilot → New campaign wizard (7 steps) → Generate plan →
Publishing Queue → approve.
**[SAY]** "Autopilot plans and schedules your content for you. Run the campaign
wizard — pick your goals, content types, frequency and destinations, and a mode.
Generate a plan and review it in the queue. In Assisted mode you approve each post;
in Full Autopilot it publishes on your schedule and reports back."

## Lesson 8 — Forge AI Assistant & guided tours · Category: Getting Started
**[ON SCREEN]** Click "Ask Forge AI" → ask a question → start a guided tour.
**[SAY]** "Stuck? Click Ask Forge AI any time. It answers questions and can launch
guided tours that highlight exactly where to click. Your first messages each month
are free."

## Lesson 9 — Dark mode & settings · Category: Getting Started
**[ON SCREEN]** Toggle theme (sun/moon), Settings page.
**[SAY]** "Prefer dark mode? Toggle it top-right anytime. That's the full loop —
create, optimize, publish, and automate. Start with a few free credits and see
what you can make."

---

## Production notes
- **Best trust:** record real screen captures (Loom/OBS) of each lesson; narrate
  with a real voice or an avatar tool (HeyGen/D-ID/Synthesia).
- Keep each clip 60–120s, 1080p, captions burned in.
- Upload finished MP4s in **Admin → Tutorials** (set category to match above) and
  they appear on `/tutorials` automatically.
- Keep the prompts/results shown on screen real — don't stage fake numbers.

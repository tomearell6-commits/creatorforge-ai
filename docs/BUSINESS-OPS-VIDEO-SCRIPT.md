# Demo Video Script — AI Business Operations Manager

Record-ready, end-to-end walkthrough of every category. Total runtime ≈ 6–7
minutes narrated. Use it two ways:

1. **Avatar video** — paste each scene's narration into Admin → Tutorials →
   "Generate avatar tutorial" (HeyGen pipeline), publish to /tutorials.
2. **Screen recording** — read the narration while following the ACTION lines.

Every claim in this script is true of the product — no promised features.

---

## Scene 1 — What it is (0:00–0:35)

**ACTION:** Open Dashboard → Business Studio → AI Business Manager.

**NARRATION:** "Meet your AI Business Operations Manager — the part of
CreatorsForge that handles your routine business admin while you stay in
control of every decision. It organizes your company information, manages your
product catalogue, triages customer inquiries, drafts replies and documents,
and scores your business health — and it never sends or publishes anything
without your approval."

## Scene 2 — Executive Dashboard (0:35–1:05)

**ACTION:** Point at Health Score, then the stat cards, then Recent AI activity.

**NARRATION:** "This is your Executive Dashboard. The Business Health Score is
calculated from your real activity — profile quality, responsiveness,
security, credits — and every point is explainable. Below it: today's
inquiries, draft replies waiting for your approval, published products,
scheduled posts, and a full audit trail of everything the AI has done."

## Scene 3 — Company Profile (1:05–1:50)

**ACTION:** Open Company Profile, fill 3–4 fields, Save, click AI Optimize.

**NARRATION:** "Start by teaching the AI your business. Company name, what you
do, who you serve, your brand voice. This profile is the AI's brain — every
reply, document and campaign it writes comes from these facts, so it never
invents claims about your business. The Optimization Score shows exactly what
to improve, and AI Optimize suggests SEO keywords and a sharper description
you can accept with one click."

## Scene 4 — Knowledge Base (1:50–2:20)

**ACTION:** Open Knowledge Base, paste an FAQ, save it.

**NARRATION:** "Then add your knowledge: FAQs, policies, pricing, brand
guidelines. When a customer asks about delivery times, the AI answers with
YOUR policy — word for word from what you stored here, not a guess."

## Scene 5 — Product Catalogue (2:20–3:00)

**ACTION:** Add a product, click "Marketing pack", scroll the results.

**NARRATION:** "Load your products, and generate a complete marketing pack for
each one: an SEO description, marketing copy, captions for four platforms, a
customer FAQ, your key differentiators — plus ready-made prompts that jump
straight into the Image and Video studios."

## Scene 6 — B2B marketplaces: the honest part (3:00–3:35)

**ACTION:** Show the "How it works" card's marketplace note; demo a copy button.

**NARRATION:** "Selling on Alibaba or GlobalSources? Those marketplaces don't
allow outside tools to manage seller accounts — so we don't pretend to.
Instead, everything they ask for — optimized company descriptions, keywords,
product listings — is generated here, and you paste each piece in with one
click. Buyer inquiries from any marketplace can be forwarded into your Inquiry
Center, where the AI takes over the triage and drafting."

## Scene 7 — Inquiry Center + AI Reply Assistant (3:35–4:30)

**ACTION:** Log an inquiry ("Quote for 100 units?"), click AI triage, then
AI draft reply, then Approve → Copy & mark sent. Log a second inquiry with
"refund" to show the red SENSITIVE flag.

**NARRATION:** "The Inquiry Center collects inquiries from your website form
or manual entry. One click and the AI classifies each one — sales, support,
quotation, partnership — with a priority and a recommendation. Then it drafts
a professional reply using your profile and knowledge. You approve, edit, or
reject; approved replies are copied to your clipboard and you send them from
your own email. Notice the refund inquiry — sensitive topics like refunds,
legal and disputes are flagged in red so you always handle them personally."

## Scene 8 — Documents (4:30–5:05)

**ACTION:** Generate a quotation with 2 line items; show the print preview.

**NARRATION:** "Need a quotation, invoice, proposal or purchase order? Give
the AI the facts and line items, and it drafts a numbered, professional
document on your letterhead — ready to review and save as PDF. It never
invents prices or legal terms; contract templates always carry a
lawyer-review note."

## Scene 9 — Marketing Center + Automation modes (5:05–5:50)

**ACTION:** Open Marketing Center, then Settings; show the three modes and the
Autopilot confirmation dialog (cancel it).

**NARRATION:** "The Marketing Center connects to your Ads studio, Autopilot
campaigns and Publishing Calendar with one workflow: create, review, approve,
schedule, publish, analyse. In Settings you pick how much the AI does alone:
Manual — you approve everything. Assisted — it prepares and notifies you.
Autopilot — only after you explicitly opt in, and even then it can only
publish content you already approved. It can never negotiate pricing, approve
refunds, accept contracts, touch billing, or send customer replies. Those are
hard limits, not settings."

## Scene 10 — Reports + Health Score (5:50–6:25)

**ACTION:** Generate a Weekly Summary; open the Health Score page.

**NARRATION:** "Finally, reporting. One click builds a business report from
your real metrics — inquiries handled, content produced, credits used — with
an AI narrative and next actions. And the Health Score page shows all eight
factors with exactly how to improve each one. That's the AI Business
Operations Manager: it does the routine, you make the decisions. Start with
your Company Profile — everything gets smarter from there."
